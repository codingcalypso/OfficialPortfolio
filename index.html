How I got started

I first learned coding in my teens when I used HTML/CSS on websites such as MySpace, Neopets, and LiveJournal. This led me to purchase my own domain where I had my own blog and honed my web design and graphic design skills. While I always dreamed of a career in development & design, life pushed me into other avenues. However, the pandemic made me re-think what I wanted out of life and instead of focusing on what I needed to do, I made the decision to focus on what I want to do. I am dedicated to making my dream a reality & I can't wait to see what I can accomplish!
