About Me

Learn more about me - Cristal!

Personal

Hi, I'm Cristal! I am a developer & designer based in the United States. I love creating various types of art whether it's graphic design, web design, or UX design. I value vulnerability, empathy, accessibility, equity, and compassion and I always try to include aspects like these in all of my design work.

I have been building websites for 20 years. They were always personal blogs (heavy utilization of HTML/CSS & Adobe PS/PSP) where I could work on my graphic design and web development skills. More recently, I have earned two certificates from Coursera in the Digital Marketing & E-Commerce certificate which gave me hands on experience utilizing Google Analytics, SEO, email marketing, and more. Then I completed the UX Design certificate where I gained hands on experience in Figma, user research, and content designs. I also completed the SheCodes workshop to gain four certificates in their Basic, Plus, Responsive, & React programs which utilized the tools of HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript & React. I am also continuously working on these skills (and others) by taking courses that help me maintain and grow my skill set.

My interests outside of design and development include reading, playing video games, and taking road trips.

If you'd like to learn more, or you like my work and would like to inquire about my services, please reach out by sending me an email!
